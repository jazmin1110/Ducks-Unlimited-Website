Privacy Policy

Plain English. We collect what we need to ship your order and keep in touch — that's it.

Last updated: 14 May 2026

What we collect

When you shop with us, we collect:

  • Your name, email, and phone (to confirm and ship your order)
  • Your shipping address (to deliver your order)
  • Order history (so we can help you with returns and exchanges)
  • Email if you sign up for our newsletter (you can unsubscribe anytime)

We do not store credit card numbers. Card details go directly to our payment processor (PayMongo) and never touch our servers.

What we do with it

  • Fulfill and ship your orders
  • Respond to your questions and process returns
  • Send order status updates and (if you've opted in) occasional newsletters about new collections
  • Aggregate sales statistics — never tied to your identity

We do not sell your data, ever.

Who we share it with

Only with the third-party services we need to run the business:

  • PayMongo — payment processing
  • Lalamove, LBC, J&T Express — order delivery
  • Supabase — our database host
  • Vercel — our website host

Each of these only sees the data they need to do their job.

Cookies

We use a small number of essential cookies to keep your cart and sign-in session working. We don't use third-party advertising trackers.

Your rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Ask us to correct anything inaccurate
  • Ask us to delete your account and personal data (we keep order records for tax purposes for 5 years per Philippine law)

Email privacy@ducksunlimited.ph for any of these.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we'll email anyone with an account before they take effect.

Questions? Email privacy@ducksunlimited.ph.
