Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

We want every customer to be able to use our site comfortably. Here's how we're getting there.

Our commitment

Ducks Unlimited is committed to making ducksunlimited.ph accessible to people with disabilities. We aim to meet the WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

What we've done

  • Semantic HTML throughout — proper headings, lists, and landmarks
  • Alt text on product photography and editorial imagery
  • Keyboard navigation: every interactive element is reachable by Tab and operable by Enter / Space
  • Visible focus indicators on links and form controls
  • Color contrast meets or exceeds WCAG AA
  • Form labels are properly associated with their inputs
  • Aria attributes on the nav, cart drawers, and accordions

What we're working on

Web accessibility is an ongoing effort. We're continuing to improve:

  • Image descriptions for product detail photography
  • Screen reader testing across the full checkout flow
  • Reduced-motion support for the hover animations

Hit a barrier?

If you encounter anything on the site that isn't accessible to you, please tell us. We treat accessibility feedback as a priority. Email accessibility@ducksunlimited.ph with:

  • The page URL where you encountered the problem
  • A description of the issue
  • The browser and assistive technology you were using, if any

We aim to respond within 2 business days and fix the issue as quickly as we can.
