Orders

How do I check the status of my order?

If you have an account, every order is listed under My Orders. We'll also email status updates as your order moves from "Awaiting Payment" through "Packed" and "Shipped."

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Reach out within 12 hours of placing the order — before we've packed it — and we can change the size, color, or cancel it entirely. After that, the order goes to our courier and you'd need to use our returns process.

I never received an order confirmation email.

Check your spam/promotions folder first. If it's not there, email us with the email and approximate time you ordered and we'll resend it.

Sizing & Fit

How do I know what size to buy?

See our Size Guide. Measure your chest first — it's the most important number for tops. If you're between sizes, size up for a more relaxed fit.

Will my polo shrink after washing?

Our Egyptian cotton is pre-shrunk, but you can expect about 2% additional shrinkage after the first cold wash. Tumble dry low to minimize it. Hang drying is best for longevity.

Care

How should I wash my Ducks Unlimited garments?

Machine wash cold with similar colors, mild detergent, no bleach. Tumble dry low or hang dry. Warm iron on the reverse side if needed. Avoid the dryer for embroidered or printed pieces.

How do I remove a stain?

For most stains: blot (don't rub), apply a small amount of mild detergent, and rinse with cold water before tossing in the wash. For oily stains, dab with a tiny bit of dish soap first.
