Customer Service

Shipping

We ship anywhere in the Philippines. Free shipping on orders over ₱2,500.

Rates & Times

Metro Manila

₱150 standard, free over ₱2,500. Delivered in 1–2 business days via Lalamove or our courier partners.

Provincial

₱250 standard, free over ₱3,500. Delivered in 3–5 business days via LBC or J&T Express.

Same-day Greenhills

Orders placed before 2:00 PM with a Greenhills / San Juan / Mandaluyong address can be delivered the same day for a ₱100 fee. Choose "Same-day local" at checkout.

Processing Times

Standard orders are packed within 1 business day. During sales or holidays, give us up to 2 business days. You'll get an email when your order ships, with tracking information.

Tracking

Once your order ships, the tracking number is added to your order page in My Orders and sent by email. Tracking links open with the relevant courier.

International

Right now we only ship within the Philippines. For international requests, email us — we can arrange forwarding to a Philippine address you choose.

Pickup

You can also pick up orders for free at our Greenhills workshop. Choose "Store pickup" at checkout and we'll have your order ready in 24 hours.
