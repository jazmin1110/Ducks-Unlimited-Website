Customer Service

Returns & Exchanges

30 days, no fuss. We want you to love what you ordered — if you don't, send it back.

What's eligible

Anything unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached, within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in resellable condition.

Not eligible: garments that have been worn, washed, altered, or had their tags removed. Gift cards are non-refundable.

How to return

  1. Email returns@ducksunlimited.ph with your order number and what you'd like to return.
  2. We'll reply within one business day with a return reference and the address to ship to.
  3. Pack the items in their original packaging with the return reference written clearly on the outside.
  4. Ship via your preferred courier. We recommend something with tracking.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the items (usually within 3 business days), your refund is issued back to your original payment method.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? Follow the steps above and mention the swap. If the new piece costs more, we'll send a payment link for the difference. If it costs less, we refund the difference.

Free exchanges if the new piece is the same price or higher — we cover the return shipping. Otherwise return shipping is on you.

Defective or wrong item

If anything arrives damaged or different from what you ordered, email us within 7 days. We'll send a replacement and arrange a free return pickup — no questions asked.

Refund timing

  • Card payments: 5–10 business days back to your card
  • GCash / GrabPay: 1–3 business days
  • Bank transfer: 3–7 business days

Questions about a return in progress? Email returns@ducksunlimited.ph with your order number.
