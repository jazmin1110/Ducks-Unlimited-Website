Fit Notes

Shorts have an 8" inseam. Trousers and chinos are sold unhemmed — we can hem them in-store at no charge while you wait.

1

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level and snug — not tight.

2

Waist

Measure around your natural waist — just above the belly button, where you'd normally sit your belt.

3

Inseam

From the top of your inner thigh down to your ankle. Best done with a pair of trousers that already fit well.
