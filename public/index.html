Our Heritage

Crafted in the Philippines since 1995

Ducks Unlimited began as a small family workshop in Greenhills, San Juan — a place to make beautiful, honest clothes the way they used to be made: slowly, by hand, with care.

Three decades on, we're still here, still vertically integrated, still finishing every hem ourselves. Because affordable luxury isn't about cutting corners. It's about clothes made to last, designed to live in, by people who care.
